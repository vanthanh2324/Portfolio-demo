Concept Game

I create concept art for the games that my school team is working on. The theme of my art is inspired by South Asian culture, especially the traditional houses from my hometown. I draw inspiration from the leaf houses, which were commonly found in Vietnam in the past century. Although these houses are not as prevalent now, you can still find a few in Vietnam.
